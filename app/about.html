Our Relationships with Amish Craftsmen Give You Affordable and Unique Handcrafted Treasures

Affordable and high-quality are not always mutually exclusive; so we learned when we visited the farms and workshops of Amish craftsmen in Pennsylvania, Ohio and Indiana. Enchanted at the quaintness and beauty of their functional works of art, we cautiously asked about pricingand were delighted with what we found! Because the Amish create such lovely, detailed and high-quality pieces, we automatically assumed we would pay dearly but much to our delight, we were wrong! We thought you would feel the same as we doand thus Cabinfield Woodworking, LLC was born.

As a national distributor based near Amish country, we bring you unique home and outdoor furnishings and accents direct from Amish workshops and farms. And because we keep merchandising, inventory and marketing costs to a minimum, were able to pass even more savings along to you. Merchandising and inventory costs are low because we dont operate from a brick and mortar location. When you place your order, we send it direct to our Amish friends who then create your pieces and send them directly to you. Marketing costs are low because we dont produce fancy brochures, send repeat mailings and hire telemarketing staff to bother you. Instead, we leverage the power of the Internet to inform and educate you. Of course should you have any questions, youre always welcome to e-mail or call.

Our Online Price Calculator Gives You Your Final Price

We love shopping online! But were always annoyed when made to guess at shipping costs, or when we must hope that the extras we want will be affordable. So at Cabinfield, weve created a unique zip code-based price calculator: you enter your zip code, and the price you see includes shipping. No hidden costs. No surprises.

Our Dedication Gives You Exceptional Service

We know how you feel about not being able to reach someone when you need answers to your questions. Our support staff is readily availableby toll-free phone during business hours, and by rapid response e-mail. We wont make you wait. Youll get the answers you need, when you need them. We truly strive to provide the best customer service in the industry, and want to hear your comments and suggestions.

Email us at service@cabinfield.com or call 866-450-WOOD (9663) anytime if you have any questions.

Thanks for visiting us. Enjoy your stay.
