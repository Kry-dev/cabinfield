With a handcrafted wood kitchen set mealtimes will become the special bonding time youve always dreamed of. Solidly constructed fan back chairs are optionally stained in 2 tones, creating natural wood and black kitchen chairs with custom contoured seats. The natural and black kitchen table features elegantly curved table legs, and is optionally an expandable kitchen table with self storing leaves.

Shown in quartersawn white oak with Michael's cherry stain. This suite includes bed, dresser with optional mirror, armoire, nightstand and blanket chest with cedar bottom.See product features and specs

Standard Features
  • Choice of wood species & stains
  • 1 thick top
  • Rounded corner top (small radius)
  • Jasper edges
  • 30" high
  • Wooden extension slides
  • Center leg on tables with 3 or more 12 leaves
  • Pewter nail heads on Collinsville chairs
  • 2 styles of chairs
  • Upholstered seats & backs (Collinsville chairs)
  • Upholstered seats (Mc Cohen chairs)
Optional Features
  • Choice of edge profiles
  • V-groove in solid tops
  • Breadboard ends
  • 1-18" butterfly leaf
  • Up to 4-12" leaves
  • Drawer in solid top table
  • Silverware drawer organizer
  • Exceptional variety of high-quality leather or fabric upholsteries
  • Nail head choices for Collinsville chairs
